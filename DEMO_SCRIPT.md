## Recording Tips

1. **Audio Quality:** Use a quiet environment and a good microphone
2. **Screen Recording:** Use OBS Studio or Camtasia for smooth recording at 1080p/60fps
3. **Pacing:** Speak clearly and pause after key points
4. **Live Data:** Ensure Binance toggle is ON for authentic real-time data
5. **Alerts:** Adjust thresholds during demo to show alert system in action
6. **Transitions:** Fade between dashboard sections for professional look
7. **Background Music:** Add subtle tech background music (optional)
8. **Subtitles:** Add captions for accessibility and engagement
